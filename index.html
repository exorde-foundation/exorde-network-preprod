fake news

We are working on developing a decentralized international and information-neutral platform that will be aimed at analyzing the spread of information on the internet. Accessible through an open-source free extension (available on all the major navigators), which does not collect data in any way. We hope to make Exorde available to all, so no one ever gets trapped into reading fake news again.

trust

Exorde is based entirely on trust. Our system is designed to be 100% transparent with its users and contributors. through its use of the blockchain. Our extension will be open source, so everyone can see that we will not be collecting their data in any way.

Furthermore, Exorde will not be ours to control. Exorde is a decentralized platform designed to be driven by its contributors. The more you contribute, the more decisions you will be able to make to help it strive.

collaborate

Exorde is a collaborative platform. It relies entirely on its contributors to create the content as we firmly believe only diverse populations (from various origins, political backgrounds, etc.) are a fit candidate for assessing the credibility of information. Users will also play an essential role as their collaboration will be critical to Exorde’s success.

It is our hope that through international collaboration we will be able to spread the use of Exorde and make of it an essential tool for navigating safely on the Internet.

neutral

Exorde is first and foremost designed to be information neutral. The systems responsible for ensuring Exorde’s neutrality with regards to information have been carefully studied to minimize the human factor with regards to political bias. Furthermore, as no system is perfect, all content generated by Exorde will be the result of the intricate collaboration of its contributors to minimize potential outliers.

contribute

Contributors are the backbone of Exorde. As they generate content for Exorde, they will be adequately rewarded in EXD. EXD is a new crypto currency, disconnected from any and all pre-existing entities, therefore ensuring that its focus remains exclusively bound to fight fake news. As Exorde’s success grows, it will become financially independent through various memberships, further strengthening the EXD, thus maintaining interest with regards to its contributors.
